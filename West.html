Historical Monuments Of World

Timeless treasures that reflect the culture, heritage, and achievements of past civilizations.

WESTERN ZONE


Chichen Itza

Location: Mexico, Yucatán Peninsula

Description:An ancient Mayan city with the iconic El Castillo pyramid.


Mount Rushmore

Location:United States, Keystone

Description: A sculpture of four U.S. presidents carved into a granite mountain.


Teotihuacan

Location: Mexico, Teotihuacan

Description:An ancient Mesoamerican city known for its massive pyramids, including the Pyramid of the Sun.


Independence Hall

Location: United-States ,Philadelphia

Description: The birthplace of the United States.


Christ the Redeemer

Location: Brazil, Rio de Janeiro

Description: A massive statue of Jesus Christ overlooking the city.


El Morro Castle

Location: Cuba , Havana

Description:A 16th-century fortress guarding the entrance to Havana Bay.


Tikal

Location:Guatemala, Petén Basin

Description: One of the largest archaeological sites of the ancient Mayan civilization.


Golden Gate Bridge

Location:United-States, California

Description:An iconic suspension bridge and a marvel of modern engineering.


La Fortaleza

Location: Puerto Rico, San Juan

Description: A historic fortress and the oldest executive mansion in continuous use in the Americas.


Nazca Lines

Location:Peru, Nazca Desert

Description: : Ancient geoglyphs etched into the desert, depicting animals and shapes, created by the Nazca culture.


Castillo de San Marcos

Location:United-States, Florida

Description: The oldest masonry fort in the continental U.S., built by the Spanish in the 17th century.


Tiwanaku

Location:Bolivia, Lake Titicaca

Description:An ancient archaeological site of a pre-Columbian civilization, known for its impressive stone structures.
